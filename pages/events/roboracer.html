ROBO RACER:

Bring out the fastest racer in you,design your bot with the fastest motors and race on!!!

TEAM SPECIFICATION

  1. Team may consist of a maximum of 4 participants.
  2. Participants from different educational institutions

           can also form a team.

Registration fee:- Rs 250/- per participant
ELIGIBILITY
Participants should carry their receipt and valid College ID
at the time of event.
MACHINE SPECIFICATION

  1. The machine should fit in a box of dimensions 250mm X 250mm.
  2. The external device used to control the machine or any external tank is    not Included in the size constraint.
  3. The machine should be controlled by a wireless remote control mechanism Or wired one throughout the race.
  4. The machine must not be made from Lego parts, or any ready-made kit, if     we find such machine it will be disqualified.
  5. The weight of machine should not exceed then 5kg.
  6. If the machine is wired then the wire should remain slack under all circumstances during the competition. All the wires coming out of the machine should be stacked as single unit .The wires should be properly insulated.

BATTERRIES & POWER

  1. The machine must be powered electrically only. Use of IC engine is not Allowed.
  2. Batteries must be sealed, immobilized electrolyte types (gel cells, lithium, NiCad, or dry cells).
  3. The electric voltage anywhere in the machine should not be more than 24V DC at any point of time.
  4. On board power supply of 24V DC will be provided.

ARENA

  1. The arena will consists of slopes (up to 30deg.), slits, rotating platform, Slips surface, muddy, water showers, rollers, sharp turnings, narrow Bridge etc.
  2. Exact track will opened on the event day, as it is subjected to change & will Be informed to participants before commencement of the event.( click here for design of track for robo race)

RULES & REGULATIONS

Game Rules:

  1. The competition is based on time trial system. There will be a qualifying round for each team.
  2. The top teams from qualifying rounds make it to the final round on basis of time trials.
  3. Five hand touches are allowed for qualification with penalty of 10secs each.
  4. Teams with top timings will be selected for final round.
  5. The machine will have to followed the provided track.
  6. If any of the machine of before start up call, the counter would be restarted at the machine will get a second chance. However, if any machine starts off before the start up call for a second time, it will be disqualified.
  7. The track will have check point at regular intervals. If a machine tumbles or halts or goes off the arena at any point on the track, one of the participants is allowed to lift it up and place it at the nearest checkpoint behind that point. The time shall still be running in the meantime.
  8. The machines are not allowed to leave any loose parts on any part of the Arena .Any machine disintegrating during the race will be disqualified.
  9. Teams are not allowed to purposefully damage the machine of the opponent' steam. If found doing so on track (while racing), the concerned team will be disqualified. Execution of this rule will be subjective and relies completely on Judge's discretion.
  10. Judges decision shall be treated as final and binding on all.

GENERAL RULES

  1. Any team that is not ready at the time specified will be eliminated from the Competition automatically.
  2. The teams must adhere to the spirit of healthy competition. The teams must not damage the opponent's machine in any way. Judges reserve the right to disqualify any team indulged in misbehavior.
  3. The name of your machine must be prominently displayed on the machine.

JUDGING CRITERIA

  1. Teams will be judge on the basis of time trials.
  2. Judges decision shall be treated as final and binding on all.

CERTIFICATION

  1. Certificate of excellence will be awarded to all winners.
  2. Participation certificates will also be given to all participants.
