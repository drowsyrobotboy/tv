PRO-DESIGNER:

Many different professions make use of computer-aided design. It is an important industrial art involved in automotive, aerospace, prosthetic, and artistic designs. The use of CAD modeling is massively widespread; anything from chairs to rockets can be designed with the aid of computer programs.

CAD modeling contest is where you bring to light all your designing talent and your knowledge about CAD software's. CAD provides you the opportunity to test your skills in the field of design. Subject your designs to real-world conditions to raise the standards of your products.

Description:

The event consists of three rounds.

First round: In the first round, the participant will be evaluated on the basis of his/her design using the 3D-CAD software. A problem statement will be given.

Second round: Participants who have cleared the first round will be admitted to the second round. Here, assessment is done on the basis of the design using CATIA software. Simple models involving part modeling will be given.

Third round: The participants who cleared the above two rounds will be given a chance to compete using their designing skills of CATIA software.

General Rules:

  • Individual participants are allowed.
  • Only a perfect CAD model will be selected for further rounds.
  • Accuracy of dimensions and the design should be maintained.

Registration:

Event registration will be done on spot.
Awards:

  • Certificate of Participation will be given to the participants' who have qualified the first round.
  • Cash prizes and merit certificates in association with ISTE will be awarded to the winners.
