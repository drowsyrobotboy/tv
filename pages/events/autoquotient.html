AUTO QUOTIENT


Let's face it; we may never see even one of the exquisite automobiles in the flesh – let alone own one – but that's true of the Mona Lisa, too. And that doesn't stop people traveling around the world to see her.
These automobiles are more than just insane pieces of engineering capable of transporting you to 60 mph faster than lightning striking a cheetah; they are cultural treasures.
We are here to test your grey matter in the ever expanding world of automobiles. Do you have the mettle to face this exhilarating world of automobiles?
Enroll and take your auto passion to full throttle!

Description:
The event consists of three rounds.
First round: Basic questions from the automobile industry.
Second round: Visual round in automobile industry for participants who clear the first round.
Third round: An even more competent round regarding the specifications of automobiles.

General Rules:

  • There is no participation fee for the first round.
  • Registration for the qualified teams of first round will be on spot.
  • A team consists of two members.

Awards:

  • Certificate of Participation will be given to the participants' who have qualified the first round.
  • Cash prizes and merit certificates in association with ISTE will be awarded to the winners.

In case of any discrepancies the decision of the coordinator and the event head shall be the final and no further arguments shall be entertained.
