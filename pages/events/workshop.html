Robotics Workshop

Bootcamps India is organizing Robotics Workshop in association with IIT Kharagpurat MGIT College. The Workshop will cover practical training in Robotics fields.

Event Highlights:

• The workshop will be in association with Spring Fest’13, IIT Kharagpur.
• The Workshop will be conducted in the different parts of the country and aims to groom the students with the basics of the field with exposure to the latest and most essential developments so far.
• The Workshop will be conducted for a period of 2 Days in MGIT College.
• Each participating student will be provided with a participation certificate.
• There will be session conducted by the experts from Bootcamps India.
• A team of six will receive a robotics kit.
• Individual participants will be grouped at the time of registration.

DATES

8th and 9th March 2014

REGISTRATION

Rs 800/- per participant
